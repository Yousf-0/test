Dish Name: Shawerma Platter

ingredients:

  • Prepare Garlic Mayo Sauce:

    • Cream ½ Cup
    • Lemon juice 3 tbs
    • Mayonnaise ½ Cup
    • Paprika powder ½ tsp
    • Himalayan Pink Salt ½ tsp or to taste
    • Sesame seeds roasted 1 & ½ tbs
    • Sugar 1 tsp
    • Fresh parsley chopped 1 tsp
    • Garlic 3 cloves
    • Prepare Spicy Tomato Sauce:
    • Onion 1 medium
    • Dried red chillies 4-5 soaked in water
    • Vinegar 1 tbs
    • Yogurt hung 1/3 Cup
    • Tomato paste 1 tbs
    • Cooking oil 2 tbs
    • Himalayan Pink Salt ½ tsp or to taste
  • Prepare Shawarma Chicken:

    • Yogurt 2 tbs
    • Paprika powder 2 tsp
    • Cinnamon powder ¼ tsp
    • Onion powder 1 tsp
    • Cumin powder ½ tsp
    • Black pepper crushed ½ tsp
    • Turmeric powder ¼ tsp
    • Coriander powder ½ tsp
    • Red chilli powder ½ tsp or to taste
    • Garlic powder 1 tsp
    • Himalayan Pink Salt 1 tsp or to taste
    • Lemon juice 2 tbs
    • Cooking oil 3-4 tbs
    • Chicken fillets ½ kg
    • Hummus
    • Iceberg lettuce shredded
    • Salad
    • Potato fries
    • Feta cheese cubes
    • Lemon juice
    • Sumac powder
    • Fresh parsley chopped
    • Pita bread grilled

Directions:

  • Prepare Garlic Mayo Sauce:

    1. In blender jug,add cream,lemon juice,mayonnaise,paprika powder,pink salt,sesame seeds,sugar,fresh parsley,garlic & blend well.White sauce is ready!
    2. Prepare Spicy Tomato Sauce:
    3. In blender jug,add onion,garlic,dried red chillies,vinegar,yogurt,tomato paste,cooking oil,pink salt & blend well.Red sauce is ready!
  • Prepare Shawarma Chicken:

    1. In frying pan,add yogurt,paprika powder,cinnamon powder,onion powder,cumin powder,black pepper crushed,turmeric powder,coriander powder,red chilli powder,garlic powder,pink salt,lemon juice, cooking oil & mix well.
    2. Add chicken & mix well,cover & marinate for 15 minutes.
    3. Turn on the flame & cook on medium flame from both sides until done (8-10 minutes) & fire roast on direct flame until its charred from all sides.
    4. Stack up chicken pieces,insert fork & cut it with the help of knife.
    5. On serving plate,add hummus,iceberg lettuce,salad,poatao fries,shawarma chicken slices,feta cheese,white sauce,red sauce,lemon juice,sumac powder,fresh parsley,pita bread & serve!
