Dish Name: Ranch Pizza

ingredients:

  • Prepare Ranch Sauce:

    • Cream: 1 cup
    • Lemon juice: 3 tablespoons
    • Mayonnaise: 1 cup
    • Black pepper powder: ½ teaspoon
    • Dried parsley: 1½ teaspoons
    • Onion powder: ½ teaspoon
    • Garlic powder: ½ teaspoon
    • Salt: 1 pinch
    • Dill (finely chopped): 1½ tablespoons

  • Prepare Pizza Dough:

    • Warm water: ½ cup
    • Warm milk: ¼ cup
    • Sugar: 1 tablespoon
    • Instant yeast: 3 teaspoons
    • All-purpose flour (sifted): 3 cups
    • Salt: 1 teaspoon
    • Cooking oil: 2 tablespoons
    • Lukewarm water: 2-3 tablespoons or as required

  • Prepare Chicken Filling:

    • Cooking oil: 2 tablespoons
    • Boneless chicken (small cubes): 400g
    • Ginger garlic paste: 1 teaspoon
    • Salt: ½ teaspoon or to taste
    • Red chilli powder: 1 teaspoon or to taste
    • Turmeric powder: ½ teaspoon
    • Cumin powder: 1 teaspoon
    • Black pepper (crushed): 1 teaspoon
    • Lemon juice: 1 tablespoon
    • Yogurt (whisked): 3 tablespoons

  • Assembling:

    • Grated cheddar cheese
    • Grated mozzarella cheese
    • Capsicum (cubes)
    • Onion
    • Tomatoes (deseeded & cubed)
    • Mushrooms (sliced)
    • Black olives (sliced)
    • Dried oregano
    • Crushed red chilli

Directions:

  1. Prepare Ranch Sauce:

    1. In a bowl, add cream and lemon juice, then whisk well.
    2. Add mayonnaise, black pepper powder, dried parsley, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and finely chopped dill. Mix until everything is well combined. The ranch sauce is ready.
  2. Prepare Pizza Dough:

    1. In a jug, add warm water, milk, sugar, and instant yeast. Mix well, cover, and let it rest for 10-15 minutes.
    2. In a bowl, add all-purpose flour and salt, then mix well.
    3. Add cooking oil and mix until the mixture becomes crumbly.
    4. Gradually add the yeast mixture while mixing.
    5. Add lukewarm water and knead until a dough is formed. Cover and let the dough proof for 1 hour in a warm place.
  3. Prepare Chicken Filling:

    1. In a frying pan, heat cooking oil and add chicken. Mix well until the chicken changes color.
    2. Add ginger garlic paste, salt, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, crushed black pepper, and lemon juice. Mix well and cook for 1-2 minutes.
    3. Add yogurt, mix well, cover, and cook on medium flame for 4-5 minutes. Then cook on high flame until the mixture dries up. Set aside.
  4. Assemble Pizza:

    1. Sprinkle dry flour and knead the dough again until smooth.
    2. Divide the dough into 3 parts. Take one part, sprinkle dry flour, and roll it out with a rolling pin.
    3. Place the rolled dough on a 9-inch pizza pan, press it gently, and prick it with a fork.
    4. Spread 3-4 tablespoons of ranch sauce, then add cheddar cheese, mozzarella cheese, cooked chicken, capsicum, onion, tomatoes, mushrooms, black olives, and more cheddar and mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle dried oregano and crushed red chilli on top. This will make 3 medium pizzas.
    5. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C for 10-12 minutes.
    6. Once baked, take the pizza out of the pan, drizzle with ranch sauce, cut into slices, and serve!
